# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free experience for
everyone who contributes to or uses Samurai.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Give and accept feedback professionally.
- Respect differing experience levels and viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or stalking.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Deliberately disruptive or abusive conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this
code of conduct and may take additional corrective action when needed.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any other official project spaces.
